Recipe #1: Chocolate Chip Cookies

  • Time: 60 min
  • Serves: 24
Ingredients Quantity
Softened Butter 1 cup
sugar 1 cup
brown sugar 1 cup
eggs 2
vanilla extract 2 teaspoons
baking soda 1 teaspoon
hot water 2 teaspoons
salt ½ teaspoon
flour 3 cups
chocolate chips 2 cups

    Directions

  • Step 1: Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
  • Step 2:Cream together the butter, white sugar, and brown sugar until smooth. Beat in the eggs one at a time, then stir in the vanilla. Dissolve baking soda in hot water. Add to batter along with salt. Stir in flour, chocolate chips, and nuts. Drop by large spoonfuls onto ungreased pans.
  • Step 3:Bake for about 10 minutes in the preheated oven, or until edges are nicely browned.

Source: Best Chocolate Chip Cookies

Recipe #2: Strawberry Pie

  • Time: 45 min
  • Serves: 8
Ingredients Quantity
pie crust, baked 1 (9inch)
fresh strawberries 1 quart
sugar 1 cup
cornstarch 3 tablespoons
water 3/4 cups
heavy whipping cream 1/2 cup

    Directions

  • Step 1: Arrange half of strawberries in baked pastry shell. Mash remaining berries and combine with sugar in a medium saucepan. Place saucepan over medium heat and bring to a boil, stirring frequently.
  • Step 2:In a small bowl, whisk together cornstarch and water. Gradually stir cornstarch mixture into boiling strawberry mixture. Reduce heat and simmer mixture until thickened, about 10 minutes, stirring constantly. Pour mixture over berries in pastry shell. Chill for several hours before serving. In a small bowl, whip cream until soft peaks form. Serve each slice of pie with a dollop of whipped cream.

Source: Strawberry Pie II

Recipe #3: Brownies

  • Time: 60 min
  • Serves: 16
Ingredients Quantity
butter 1/2 cup
sugar 1 cup
eggs 2
vanilla extract 2 teaspoons
unsweetened cocoa powder ⅓ cup
flour 1/2 cup
salt 1/4 teaspoon
baking powder 1/4 teaspoon

    Directions

  • Step 1: Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease and flour an 8-inch square pan.
  • Step 2: In a large saucepan, melt 1/2 cup butter. Remove from heat, and stir in sugar, eggs, and 1 teaspoon vanilla. Beat in 1/3 cup cocoa, 1/2 cup flour, salt, and baking powder. Spread batter into prepared pan.
  • Step 3: Bake in preheated oven for 25 to 30 minutes. Do not overcook.

Source: Best Brownies
